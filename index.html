Welcome To Melly!

We connect patients to medical practioners at no charge. Talk to medical practitioners about mental, physical, emotional, and spiritual health. Discuss your problems and get proffesional reccomendations.

Patient Practitioner
For Patients:

If you are a patient looking for quick and confidential advice about health or mental health, this is a place to connect with a caring practitioner who is there to listen at no charge.

For Practioners:

If you are a practitioner sign up to offer free assistance to those in need. All credentials will be verified.

  • Get Verified

    To sign up as a medical practioner, you must upload photocopies of your medical license, degree and other important credentials. Our team at Melly will then verify your credentials.

  • Volunteering

    Melly provides the platform to volunteer to help those in need of your service. Form virtual connections with others across the globe.

  • Reach Out

    If you have any questions or comments, please contact us here.
